Questions

Good to know

How is the "best pick" chosen?

GasMeUp doesn't just rank by lowest price. It factors in how far you'd actually drive, so a slightly pricier station that's close can beat a cheaper one across town. You always see the full list too — the crown is just our math on which stop is worth it.

Do I need an account?

No. There's no sign-up wall and no email required to use the app. You set your area and start scanning.

Where do the prices come from?

From live, third-party fuel-price data for stations near you. Prices can change quickly, so always confirm at the pump — GasMeUp points you to the best-value option, it doesn't guarantee a specific price.

What's Highway Mode?

On a long drive, GasMeUp can scan the stations along the road ahead instead of just around your current spot — so you can choose the right exit to fill up at before you're running low.
